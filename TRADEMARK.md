The **names and branding** are a separate matter. "Pennyworth"™ and "Alfred"™,
together with the Alfred portrait/avatar, are used as project and product names
by Haim Elbaz. The open-source licence covers the code, **not** these names.

In practice:

- ✅ Fork the code, build on it, ship your own product from it.
- ✅ Say "built on Pennyworth" or "powered by Pennyworth" to credit the origin.
- ❌ Don't release your fork or a competing product *as* "Alfred" or "Pennyworth",
  or use the branding in a way that implies it's the official project or is
  endorsed by Haim Elbaz.

If you redistribute a modified version, please give it your own name. This keeps
users from confusing your build with the original — and keeps the original
clearly attributable to its author.
