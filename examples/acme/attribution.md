## Attribution & Identity

When committing or opening pull requests on the Acme platform, author commits as
the bot identity **`acme-bot <bot@acme.example>`** — never as the human at the
keyboard — so automation is clearly attributed. Credit the person who requested
the work with an `Engineered by <name email>` line in the PR body, and keep the
`Co-Authored-By` trailer for Pennyworth.

For cloud operations, use the `acme-dev` profile for everyday work and never a
production profile without explicit sign-off.
